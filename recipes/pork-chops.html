Pork Chops Recipe

Home

Description

A quick and simple grilled pork chop that everyone will love featuring a simple and easy glaze made with ketchup, honey, soy sauce, and garlic.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup ketchup
  • 2 2/3 tablespoons honey
  • 2 tablespoons low-sodium soy sauce
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 6 (4 ounce) 1 inch thick pork chops

Steps

  1. Preheat grill for medium heat and lightly oil the grate. Gather ingredients?
  2. Whisk ketchup, honey, soy sauce, and garlic together in a bowl to make a glaze.
  3. Sear the pork chops on both sides on the preheated grill. Lightly brush glaze onto each side of the chops as they cook; grill until no longer pink in the center, about 7 to 9 minutes on per side. An instant-read thermometer in started into the center should read 145 degrees F (63 degrees C)
  4. Serve hot and enjoy!
